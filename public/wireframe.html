Explore the Disney Princesses

Belle Rapunzel Ariel Jasmine Tiana

Belle

Biography: Belle is a kind and intelligent young woman who loves books and dreams of adventure beyond her small town.

Story Highlights: She learns to look beyond appearances and discovers the importance of love, courage, and compassion.

Fun Fact: Belle's love for reading inspired fans around the world to embrace learning.

Rapunzel

Biography: Rapunzel is a spirited and creative princess with magical hair that holds healing powers.

Story Highlights: She escapes her tower, discovers the world, and learns the importance of freedom and self-discovery.

Fun Fact: Rapunzel’s tower was inspired by traditional European folklore.

Ariel

Biography: Ariel, the adventurous mermaid princess, dreams of life on land and exploring the human world.

Story Highlights: She trades her voice to live on land and learns about love, identity, and sacrifice.

Fun Fact: Ariel’s curiosity often leads her to brave adventures that inspire courage in others.

Jasmine

Biography: Princess Jasmine is independent, strong-willed, and always seeks to shape her own destiny.

Story Highlights: She challenges societal norms and chooses love on her own terms.

Fun Fact: Jasmine was one of the first Disney princesses to challenge traditional gender roles.

Tiana

Biography: Tiana is hardworking and ambitious, dreaming of opening her own restaurant in New Orleans.

Story Highlights: She learns the value of friendship, love, and staying true to her dreams.

Fun Fact: Tiana is Disney’s first African-American princess, inspiring representation and diversity.
